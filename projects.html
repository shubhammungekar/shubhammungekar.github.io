15 kW CLLC Resonant DC-DC Converter

Designed and experimentally validated a 600 V – 28 V resonant DC-DC converter for heavy-duty military auxiliary power module applications.

  • 97% peak efficiency at 10 kW
  • 82 W/in³ power density
  • MIL-STD EMI compliance validation
